Our Products

Q-Tech provides a wide-ranging portfolio of equipment and spares for Thermal Power Plants, Distillery, Cement, Steel, Sugar and Mining Plants according to the requirements of customers. These equipment are pollution free and manufactured by using the high quality raw material from certified vendors to meet the expectations of our customers.
All of our equipment & spare parts go through strict inspection and quality checks to avoid any fitment or functional issues at our client end.
Some of the qualitative features of our equipment are:
  • Aesthetically good finish.
  • High quality at low cost.
  • Minimum delivery time.
  • Customized design.
  • High performance.
  • Low maintenance.
  • Ease of use.
  • Reliability.
